How to play

To play this game, you and your oponent must play a card each turn. To win you have to finish all your cards before your oponent, or have less cards than your oponent when the deck is empty.

You play one card each turn. You have to place the card wherever you think the value of your card is greater or less than the one in the table. If you are right, the card stays in position and the turn passes to your opponent. If not, the card is positioned in the correct spot on the table and you draw a card from de deck.
